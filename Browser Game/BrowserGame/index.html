Coin Collector

Welcome to the game, it is a simple game. As the title suggests, you just collect as much coins as possible.

Details & Instructions

  • Collect as many coins as possible
  • Move using the arrow keys
  • Silver spinning blades will deal 1 damage upon hit
  • 8 Pink spinning blades will spawn in after you collect 10 coins, it will deal 3 times the damage upon hit
  • There is no time limit or collecting limit, just survive and collect as many coins as you can
  • 10 Pink spinning blades will spawn in after you collect 20 coins, it will deal the same damage as the old pink blade upon hit
  • It is an endless game mode
